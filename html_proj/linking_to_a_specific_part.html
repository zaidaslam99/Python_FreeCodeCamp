Arc Shot

A shot in which the subject is photgraphed by an encircling or moving camera

Interlude

A brief, intervening film sene or sequnece, not specifically tied to the plot, that appears within the film

Prologue

A speech, preface, introduction, or brief scene precedding the main action or plot of a file; contrast to an epilogue
